Calypso Coffee

Description

A perfect drink for a cold winter day, Calypso Coffee's sweeet chocolate taste will always leave you satisfied.

And its best feature: it's so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 fluid ounce (30 ml.) rum
  • 1 fluid ounce (30 ml.) creme de cacao liqueur
  • 8 fluid ounce (240 ml.) hot brewed coffee

Steps

  1. Pour rum and liquor into a mug
  2. Fill mug to the top with coffee
