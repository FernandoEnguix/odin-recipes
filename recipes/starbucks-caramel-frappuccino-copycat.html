Starbucks® Caramel Frappuccino Copycat Recipe

Description

Have you ever wondered how do they pull off such amazing drinks in there?

Well... look no further, we got you covered!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice
  • 1 cup low-fat milk
  • 1/3 cup caramel sauce
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Blend ice, coffee, milk, caramel sauce and sugar together in a blender, on high, until smooth.
  2. Pour drink into two 16-ounce (450 gr.) glasses.
